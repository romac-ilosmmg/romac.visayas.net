Romac Visayas Services Inc.

ROMAC, a company with a 41-year history, was founded in September 1980. Initially awarded small contracts by the 13th Air Force Contracting Center of the U.S. Air Force at Clark Air Base, ROMAC focused on repairing equipment for various U.S. government agencies stationed at the base. In December 1995, ROMAC VISAYAS was established, operating in Iloilo and Bacolod before expanding to Cebu and Boracay. Known for its dependable manpower services in the Visayas Region, the company excels in Janitorial Services and has catered to esteemed companies in production and sales utilities. In the Visayas region, ROMAC is the top choice for businesses in need of manpower services, including prestigious five-star hotels in Boracay. Offering a range of services and positions such as Janitorial, Clerical, Merchandising, Front-liners, Bakers, Baristas, Bartenders, Housekeeping attendants, Drivers, Boat Men, Messengers, and more, ROMAC VISAYAS not only provides training and job opportunities but also aims to improve the lives of its employees, enabling them to support their families. With a personalized approach, ROMAC values both its clients and its dedicated employees.


Mission

To become instruments of Human Development, we commit to:

• Become the lead institution in providing quality manpower services to our clients
- Face the challenges arising in technology for the third millennium.
- Service the needs of our clients at all times.
- Develop in every person the love for their work.
- Be role models of quality service where each one is responsible for the uniqueness of every person.

• Become mirrors of truth and justice to every person
- Use objective and humanitarian reason as a tool in making judgments and decisions.
- Accept criticisms and learn from them.
- Respect the rights of individuals.

• Provide and seek opportunities for people to grow and touch their lives
- Boost the morale and draw out inherent talents and skills of each one.
- Train people to be more sufficient and efficient in order to cope with their life's challenges.

• Become responsible leaders in society
- Protect our company as a successful instrument for human development through each one's witness to life.
